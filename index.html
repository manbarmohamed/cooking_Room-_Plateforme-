Here's a simple recipe with basic ingredients for scrambled eggs:
  • Ingrediants
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • Butter or cooking oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Optional: chopped herbs like parsley or chives,
    shredded cheese, diced vegetables like tomatoes or bell peppers
  1. Instructions:
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them
    until the yolks and whites are well combined.
  3. Season the beaten eggs with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet
    over medium heat until it's melted and hot.
  5. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them
    cook for a few seconds until the edges start to set.
  6. Serve the scrambled eggs hot, optionally garnished with chopped herbs,
    shredded cheese, or diced vegetables if desired.
